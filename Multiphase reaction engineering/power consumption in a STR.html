power consumption in a stirred tank reactor

  1. estimate impeller Re
    Let impeller be a pitched blade turbine with is 2, N=180 rpm = 3rps,
    = 800mm, =1600mm, =1000 kg/m^3

    Now power = Np(N^3)D^5
    power 17.7 kW
    we usually account 15-20% losses
    so motor requirement= 22.1 kW
    so in horsepower P=P (in W) 746
    P= 29.6 hp
    so we assume 30 hp

  2. for =1000, =5000 cP= 5 Pa.s
    Np =15
    so P= 132.7 kW
    actual P= 165.9 kW
    so hp req= 222.4 hp
    for high viscocity liqs agitation speed is maintained bet 10-30 rpm ie close clearance impellers

  3. anchor impellers
    N=24 rpm= 0.4 rps, Di/Dt = .95, =1000kg/m^3
    = 5 Pa.s
    Re= 184
    Np= 3
    P= 1557.83 W
    P= 1947.3 W
    P= 2.6 hp (5 hp motor)
