5. RDGA

for a particular transfer function one should be able to find gain and A.R. & phase lag for various different frequencies.

  • first make a circle of AR put tgt by the diagonal elements at the initial frequency
  • non diagonal elements -> put tgt find phase lag and AR
  • at that frequency, draw circle of radius AR of the non diagonal elements.
  • we keep on doing this till the last circle reaches 0.
  • this is known as Gershgorin Band
